About Savanna's Coffee House

Who We Are

Founded was in 2006 by MSU graduate students Michel Bohmer and Sandy Thomas. Located in the heart of MSU’s campus town, Savanna’s is designed to be a funky destination. We serve premium products such as fresh roasted coffee and espresso drinks, hot and cold teas, grilled and cold sandwiches, and homemade soups. Not just a daytime destination! Stop by in the evening, enjoy a glass of wine or beer and listen to some live entertainment in our connected theatre space!

The Vibe

Step inside and you'll find artsy decor, cozy seating, and a relaxed atmosphere that invites you to linger. Whether you're catching up with friends, studying for exams, or simply enjoying a quiet moment, Savanna's is the perfect spot to unwind.

Our Menu

We take pride in serving fresh roasted coffee and crafted espresso drinks made from the finest beans around the world. Not a coffee person? We've got you cover with a variety of hot and iced teas, smoothies, and refreshers.

Hungry? Try one of our delicious grilled or cold sandwiches, homemade soups, and pastries baked fresh daily. Visit our Menu page to explore all the tasty options we offer.

Night Life & Events

Savanna's isn't just your morning coffee stop - it transforms at night into a live entertainment lounge with performances from local musicians, acoustic nights, open mic sessions, poetry slams, and more. Check out our Events page for the latest schedule and join us for a night of fun and creativity.

Built for Students

We understand the student life. That's why we offer:

  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi
  • Charging stations at every table
  • Quiet study zones
  • 10% off with valid MSU IS
